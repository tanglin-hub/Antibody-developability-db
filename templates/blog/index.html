About Antibody Database

The Antibody Database serves as a groundbreaking resource in the field of antibody drug design, where accurately predicting antibody properties is essential for selecting optimal candidate drugs and enhancing therapeutic efficacy. It compiles experimentally determined physicochemical properties of antibodies, providing invaluable references for antibody drug development. To date, the database has accumulated information on 1,302 antibodies, including 918 sequences and 6,772 distinct antibody properties, supporting the advancement of antibody-based research and drug design.Learn more >
